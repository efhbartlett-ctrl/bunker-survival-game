# Bunker Survival Game

A first-person 3D post-apocalyptic bunker management game.

## Play Online

Visit: https://efhbartlett-ctrl.github.io/bunker-survival-game/

## Controls

- **WASD** - Move around the bunker
- **Mouse** - Look around
- **Space** - Jump
- **Q** - Complete quests
- **P** - Pause the game

## Game Objective

Maintain your underground bunker by completing quests. Manage four critical resources:

- **Power** - Keeps the bunker operational
- **Water** - Essential for survival
- **Food** - Sustains the population
- **Morale** - Keeps residents happy

Resources degrade over time. Complete quests to restore them before they reach critical levels!

## Features

- First-person 3D exploration
- Real-time resource management
- Quest-based gameplay
- Dynamic bunker environment

## Technology

Built with **Babylon.js** 3D engine and vanilla JavaScript.
